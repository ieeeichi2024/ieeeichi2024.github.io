CALL FOR SPONSORS

The ICHI 2022 organizing committee invites interested parties to participate in its sponsorship and support program. We are delighted to offer conference sponsorship packages. If you do not find the perfect package for you below, please contact us to customize a sponsorship package for your organization.

Platinum, Gold, Silver and Bronze Sponsor Packages

Platinum: $5,000
  • Advance recognition as a Platinum Sponsor in electronic communications and on the web.
  • Recognized as a Platinum Sponsor on the conference program.
  • Three 1-day complimentary registrations for the conference.
  • One complimentary industry booth space.
Gold: $3,000
  • Advance recognition as a Gold Sponsor in electronic communications and on the web.
  • Recognized as a Gold Sponsor on the conference program.
  • Two 1-day complimentary registration for the conference.
  • One complimentary industry booth space.
Silver: $1,500
  • Advance recognition as a Silver Sponsor in electronic communications and on the web.
  • Recognized as a Silver Sponsor on the conference programm.
  • One 1-day omplimentary registration for the conference.
Bronze: $500
  • Advance recognition as a Bronze Sponsor in electronic communications and on the web.
  • Recognized as a Bronze Sponsor on the conference program.

Other Sponsorship Packages

Student and conference best paper awards: $1,500
  • Advance recognition as a Best Paper Awards Sponsor in electronic communications and on the web.
  • Recognized as a Best Paper Awards Sponsor on the conference program.

Event Sponsor

The organizing committee also invites interested parties to support social events of the meeting, such as coffee breaks, lunch or dinner banquets. Supporters will receive:

  • Advance recognition as the Event's Sponsor in electronic communications and on the web.
  • Recognized as the Event's Sponsor on the conference program.
